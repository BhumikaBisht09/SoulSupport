Mental Health Support for Teens (13-19)

You’re not alone. Many teens feel overwhelmed by school, relationships, or expectations. It’s okay to ask for help.

Helpful Tips

  • Talk to someone you trust
  • Get regular sleep and eat well
  • Spend time away from screens
  • Write your thoughts in a journal
  • Practice deep breathing or mindfulness

Where to Get Help

Reach out to a school counselor, a parent, or a therapist. You can also call a local helpline or mental health center.
